Pizza

Description

Delicious homemade pizza with fresh toppings.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Cheese
  • Pepperoni
  • Vegetables

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 450°F (230°C).
  2. Roll out dough and spread tomato sauce.
  3. Add cheese and toppings.
  4. Bake for 15 minutes or until golden.

Lasagna

Description

This classic lasagna recipe is rich, hearty, and full of flavor.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cups marinara sauce
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 2 cups mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  3. In a pan, sauté onion and brown the beef.
  4. Layer noodles, sauce, and cheese in a baking dish.
  5. Bake for 45 minutes or until bubbly.
